SDR Capacity Utilization

Track how much of each SDR's working hours are accounted for by logged activity. Surfaces under-utilized SDRs (coverage capacity), over-utilized SDRs (burnout/quality risk), and pairs utilization with output to distinguish execution issues from healthy hustle.

Overview

This Skill builds a dashboard grounded in your connected GTM data, with 7 widgets that answer the questions below. Build time on typical data: ~10 min.

What you'll get

Sage assembles a dashboard in roughly ~10 min covering 7 distinct widgets. Every value is source-linked back to 3 integrations and computed using your team's canonical definitions.

Questions answered

This Skill is designed to answer 4 concrete questions GTM teams ask repeatedly:

  • Do we have enough SDR capacity for current lead volume?
  • Which SDRs are at burnout-risk vs under-utilized?
  • Which SDRs are highly utilized but low-output (execution issue)?
  • How does utilization compare across teams?

Before we run

Sage proposes a complete plan and asks you to confirm 3 inputs before any query executes. Defaults are applied if you accept the plan as-is — you can override any of them or set the override as your new canonical.

How it works

A 4-step flow from your connected data to a verified, source-linked output.

01

Connect

Sage reads from your existing source systems — Salesforce, Outreach, Gong. No ETL, schema changes, or warehouse builds required. The data stays where it lives; Sage authenticates and queries it in place.

02

Confirm

Sage proposes a plan and surfaces 3 inputs for you to confirm (listed under "Inputs required" below). You approve or modify the plan. Nothing executes until you sign off.

03

Compute

Sage runs the SQL queries, joins, and Python transforms, applying your team's canonical KDs (Key Definitions) — your ROAS formula, your attribution model, your stage definitions. Every metric uses your math, not a textbook default.

04

Render

Output is generated as a dashboard with 7 widgets (listed under "Outputs generated" below). Every number is source-linked — click any value to see the SQL issued, the rows that contributed, and the formula applied.

Inputs required

The 3 parameters Sage will confirm before running:

  • 1.Time window — trailing 4 or 8 weeks?
  • 2.Working hours per SDR per week — 40, 35, or tenant-configured?
  • 3.Healthy utilization range — 40–80%, or override?
